About

Run by Nyangbe Barnes, here in Champaign County

Nyangbe Cleaning Services is a locally owned cleaning company serving businesses and homes across Champaign County, Illinois. We handle the work most providers split up: recurring commercial cleaning, everyday residential cleaning, and the heavy cleanup that comes after construction and remodels. One call covers all three.

The company is owned and run by Nyangbe Barnes, who built it on a simple idea: show up on schedule, pay attention to the details, and leave every space easy to keep up. Whether it is an office that needs nightly upkeep or a home that needs a deep clean, you work directly with an owner who stands behind the result.
