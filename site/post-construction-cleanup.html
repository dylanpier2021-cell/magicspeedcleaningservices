Good to know

Common questions

When should the cleanup happen?

After the final construction work is complete and major tools and materials are out of the space. I can coordinate timing with your contractor so the cleanup lands right before move in.

Do you handle both homes and commercial builds?

Yes. I clean up after residential renovations and new homes as well as newly built or remodeled business spaces across Champaign County.

How is post-construction cleaning priced?

It depends on the size of the space and how much dust and debris is left behind. Every job is custom, so share the details and I return a clear quote within 24 hours.

Can you remove leftover debris?

I clear the dust, packaging, and light debris that comes with a finished build. For large material haul away, let me know in advance so I can plan for it in your quote.
