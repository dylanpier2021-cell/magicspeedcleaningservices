Good to know

Common questions

Can you clean outside our business hours?

Yes. Early mornings, evenings, and weekends are all options. We plan the schedule so cleaning never interrupts your customers or your team.

Do you offer recurring service or just one time visits?

Both. Most businesses choose a recurring schedule for consistency, but we are glad to handle one time cleans, seasonal resets, or special events too.

How is commercial pricing determined?

It depends on your square footage, the scope you need, and how often we visit. Every job is custom, so share those details and we return a clear quote within 24 hours.

Will the same people clean our space each time?

We keep a consistent crew on your account whenever possible. Familiar faces mean they learn your space, your priorities, and your security routine.
