Good to know

Common questions

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Not at all. Many clients give us a preferred entry method and go about their day. Others like to be home the first time so we can learn their preferences together. Whatever feels comfortable for you works for us.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes, we arrive ready to work with our own supplies and equipment. If you prefer specific products for your home, let us know in advance and we are glad to use them.

How much does a house cleaning cost?

Every home is different, so pricing is quote based rather than one size fits all. Share a few details about your space and what you would like cleaned, and we send a fair, honest quote within 24 hours.

Can I have the same cleaner each time?

We keep familiar faces on your home whenever possible, so the people in your space already know how you like things done.
