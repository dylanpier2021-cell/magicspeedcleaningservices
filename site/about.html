Our story

Eighteen years of caring for local homes and businesses.

Magic Speed Cleaning Services started with one person, a bucket of supplies, and a belief that has not changed since: a clean space should feel cared for, not just cleaned.

Get a Free Quote Where We Work

It began at someone's front door

Eighteen years ago, Nyangbe knocked on her first door with a simple offer: let me clean your home the way I would clean my own. That home turned into another, then a small business down the road, and slowly into a steady list of families and owners who knew they could count on her.

She built this company from the ground up, one honest job at a time. There was no franchise handbook and no shortcuts. There was just the work, done carefully, and the trust that grew every time she showed up when she said she would.

I clean every home and business like it belongs to my own family, because to the people who live and work there, it does.

What started as a one-person effort is now a licensed Illinois business, fully insured through Pekin Insurance. The scale has grown. The promise behind it has not.

What stays the same, every visit

We are still family run and proudly local. We are not a call center routing jobs to strangers. When you reach out, you are talking to the people who will actually be in your space, learning how you like things, and remembering it the next time.

Care, reliability, and respect are not slogans here. They are why people have stayed with us for years and sent their neighbors our way. We would rather grow slowly and keep that trust than rush and lose it.
