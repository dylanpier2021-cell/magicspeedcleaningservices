03 / Post-construction cleanup

The build is finished, now let us make it shine

New construction and remodels leave a layer of fine dust in every corner. We do the careful, detailed cleanup that turns a finished job site into a space that is truly ready to live or work in.

Dust & debris removal

  • Fine drywall and sanding dust cleared
  • Leftover debris and packaging removed
  • Vents, ledges, and edges detailed

Surfaces & fixtures

  • Counters, cabinets, and shelving wiped inside and out
  • Light fixtures, switches, and outlets cleaned
  • Doors, frames, and trim detailed

Floors & final detail

  • Floors vacuumed, swept, and mopped
  • Windows, sills, and tracks cleaned
  • Final walk through for the finishing touches

We work with homeowners after a renovation, builders and contractors handing over a finished job, and business owners opening a newly built space. Fresh paint, new flooring, and just installed fixtures get a gentle, mindful hand.

Service questions

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes, we arrive ready to work with our own supplies and equipment. If you prefer specific products for your home or business, let us know in advance and we are glad to use them.

Do I need to be there during the cleaning?

Not at all. Many clients give us a preferred entry method and go about their day. Others like to be there the first time so we can learn their preferences together.

How is pricing decided?

Every job is custom, so pricing is quote based rather than one size fits all. It depends on the size and condition of the space, the type of cleaning, and how often we visit. We send a clear quote within 24 hours.

Can I combine services?

Absolutely. Many clients pair a one-time deep clean or post-construction cleanup with ongoing recurring service. Tell us your goals and we build a plan around them.
